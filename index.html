🌍 Our Story

More Than an NGO —
A Family of Changemakers

Ana Khayyak was founded in 2017 with a simple yet powerful vision: to build hope and create positive change in Lebanese communities through the power of youth volunteerism. What started as a small group of passionate individuals has grown into a vibrant community of over 30 dedicated volunteers, working across different regions, faiths, and backgrounds to serve those in need.

💡 Who We Are

Our Mission, Vision & Values

The principles and purpose that guide every action we take in Lebanese communities.

🎯
Our Mission

To empower youth through volunteer opportunities that foster personal development, social cohesion, and community impact. We create spaces for meaningful engagement, dialogue, and action that address the needs of vulnerable populations across Lebanon.

🌟
Our Vision

A Lebanon where every individual, regardless of their background, has access to opportunities for growth, connection, and meaningful contribution to their communities — a society built on mutual respect, understanding, and solidarity.

🌍
Our Approach

We collaborate with universities, foundations, and organizations across Lebanon to implement evidence-based programs. Our partnerships amplify our reach, connecting students, young professionals, and community leaders to drive real, lasting change.

The Values We Live By

❤️
Love
Building connections through compassion and genuine care for every person we serve.
🌱
Hope
Inspiring positive change in every community, even in the most challenging of circumstances.
⚡

Taking courageous steps towards a better future, challenging the status quo with conviction.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
Siblinghood
Creating bonds that transcend differences — of faith, background, and geography — across all of Lebanon.
